Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for funding?

Any student enrolled in a recognized institution in Nigeria can apply for scholarships or loans through MEX FUND.

Is there a fee to use MEX FUND?

No, MEX FUND is completely free for students seeking funding opportunities.

How do I know if I qualify?

Our platform matches you with opportunities based on your profile, GPA, course, and special categories.

How soon will I get a response?

Most students receive recommendations instantly after submitting their profile. Application responses depend on the funding provider.
